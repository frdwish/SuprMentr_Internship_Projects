Assignment Name: Storytelling with Graphs

Internship Diary Entry
Description of the Assignment:

On this day, I worked on creating different types of data visualizations to understand how graphs help in storytelling.

The task was to create:

A Bar Chart
A Pie Chart
A Histogram

After creating the charts, I had to write a short data story explaining the trends shown in the graphs.

This assignment helped me understand how visual representation makes data easier to interpret and communicate.


Short Data Story:

The graphs show that Computer and Science have the highest marks, while History has the lowest. The pie chart highlights that Computer contributes the largest percentage. The histogram shows most marks fall between 70–95, indicating overall good performance.
