Assignment Name: Customer Segmentation

Internship Diary Entry
Description of the Assignment:

On this day, I worked on Customer Segmentation using the K-Means clustering algorithm.
The objective of this assignment was to group customers based on their behavior and spending patterns.

I used a sample mall dataset containing features like Annual Income and Spending Score. Using K-Means clustering, I divided customers into different groups such as:

High income, high spending
High income, low spending
Low income, high spending
Low income, low spending

This helped in understanding how businesses can target different types of customers for marketing.
