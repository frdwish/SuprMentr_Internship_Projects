Assignment Name: Decision Tree on Paper

Internship Diary Entry
Description of the Assignment:

On this day, I worked on understanding how a Decision Tree works by designing a simple model on paper.

The task was to create a decision tree to predict whether a person should play outside or not based on conditions such as:

Weather (Sunny/Rainy)
Temperature (Hot/Cool)
Humidity
Wind

I structured the decision-making process step by step, where each condition leads to a decision.

This assignment helped me understand how decision trees split data based on conditions and how they are used in classification problems.
